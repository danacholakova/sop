My dearest Bash,

You are the most honest man I know. You share every part of yourself with me (ls) and never forget anything (ctrl+r). You are flexible with me taking it step-by-step (ctrl+f) or sometimes taking the plunge and going till the end (ctrl+e). If I ever need you to … we can always take a step backwards (ctrl+b) or start at the beginning (ctrl+a) You never get stuck on one thing, and can show me everything I ever want to see (cd). You’re the best for being honest with me, thank you for showing me even your deepest secrets (ls –a)!
Love,
Dana

  • man : pull up the manual
  • ls : list of the current directory’s content
  • ctrl+r : searches backward
  • ctrl+f : moves forward one character
  • ctrl+e : moves to the end of line
  • … : go back to previous directory
  • ctrl+b : moves backwards one letter
  • ctrl+a : moves to the beginning of a line
  • cd : change directory
  • ls –a : lists all files, including hidden ones
